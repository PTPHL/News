9-9-2017 Player of the week: Viktor Arvidsson (R)

A native of Sweden, Viktor Arvidsson was originally drafted to the NHL by the Nashville Predators in the 4th round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 144 NHL games during his career, notching 41 goals and adding 40 assists along with 65 penalty minutes. The 24 year old right wing has played 2 games with the Nashville Predators this season, totalling 2+2=4 with 2 PIM.
