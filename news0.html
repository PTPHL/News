13-2-2020 NHL All-Star Skill Competitions
The annual All-Star event kicked off today with the skill competitions. Team North America and Team World competed in six different races and the fans got to see their stars shine.

Team World won the puck control relay with a team of Alex Ovechkin, Erik Karlsson and William Nylander.

Patrick Kane of Team North America won the fastest skater competition, beating Nicklas Bäckström in the final.

Kyle Okposo of Team North America won the hardest shot competition with a result of 84 MPH, beating Nino Niederreiter in the final.

Sidney Crosby of Team North America won the accuracy shooting competition, beating Patrik Laine in the final.

Antti Raanta of Team World won the goalie competition (pass&score), beating Carey Price in the final.

Team North America won the breakaway relay with a team of Connor McDavid, Sidney Crosby and Patrick Kane.

The yearly clash between Team North America and Team World will take place tomorrow.
