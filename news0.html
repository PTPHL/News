9-2-2018 NHL All-Star Skill Competitions
The annual All-Star event kicked off today with the skill competitions. Team North America and Team World competed in six different races and the fans got to see their stars shine.

Team North America won the puck control relay with a team of Patrick Kane, Tyler Seguin and Steven Stamkos.

Patrick Kane of Team North America won the fastest skater competition, beating Alexander Wennberg in the final.

Brent Seabrook of Team North America won the hardest shot competition with a result of 85 MPH, beating Rasmus Ristolainen in the final.

Victor Hedman of Team World won the accuracy shooting competition, beating Patrick Kane in the final.

Cory Schneider of Team North America won the goalie competition (pass&score), beating Andrei Vasilevski in the final.

Team North America won the breakaway relay with a team of Patrick Kane, Steven Stamkos and Tyler Seguin.

The yearly clash between Team North America and Team World will take place tomorrow.
