5-12-2017 Jonathan Drouin injured.
Jonathan Drouin (MON) suffered ankle injury during the game in an incident with Mark Borowiecki (OTT). He is expected to be sidelined for more than a month. 1-2 months of no hockey will be harder to deal with than this injury believe me, he said in a TV interview.
3-12-2017 Justin Williams reaches a milestone !
Justin Williams(CAR) scored the 700th point of his career in tonight's game against the Buffalo Sabres on a goal by Jordan Staal. I'm not out here to set records, but it's always nice to get the points, he replied before ducking into the dressing room..

A native of Canada, Justin Williams was originally drafted to the NHL by the Philadelphia Flyers in the 1st round of the 2000 entry draft. He has appeared in 1104 NHL games during his career, notching 279 goals and adding 421 assists along with 690 penalty minutes. The 36 year old right wing has played 24 games with the Carolina Hurricanes this season, totalling 6+12=18 with 30 PIM.
