4-12-2017 Nikita Gusev frustrated
Vegas Golden Knights left wing Nikita Gusev feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Russia, Nikita Gusev was originally drafted to the NHL by the Tampa Bay Lightning in the 7th round of the 2012 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 25 year old left wing has not yet played for the Vegas Golden Knights this season. He has played 25 games with the Chicago Wolves this season, totalling 13+14=27 with 10 PIM.
