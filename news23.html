12-1-2018 Artturi Lehkonen reaches career high !
Artturi Lehkonen marked a new career high in assists with 11 assists after assisting in tonights game against Winnipeg Jets on a goal by Jack Roslovic. I just come to the rink ready to play and give it 110%, he joked.

A native of Finland, Artturi Lehkonen was originally drafted to the NHL by the Montreal Canadiens in the 2nd round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 114 NHL games during his career, notching 30 goals and adding 21 assists along with 28 penalty minutes. The 22 year old left wing has played 41 games with the Vegas Golden Knights this season, totalling 12+11=23 with 20 PIM.
