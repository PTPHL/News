1-1-2018 William Carrier frustrated
Tampa Bay Lightning right wing William Carrier feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, William Carrier was originally drafted to the NHL by the St. Louis Blues in the 2nd round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 80 NHL games during his career, notching 10 goals and adding 7 assists along with 54 penalty minutes. The 23 year old right wing has played 39 games with the Tampa Bay Lightning this season, totalling 5+4=9 with 33 PIM.
