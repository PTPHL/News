6-10-2017 Ilya Kovalchuk reaches a milestone !
Ilya Kovalchuk notched the 400th assist of his career in tonight's game against the Los Angeles Kings on a goal by Ondrej Palat. My kids attended this game so it is extra special for me, the player said in an interview..

A native of Russia, Ilya Kovalchuk was originally drafted to the NHL by the Columbus Blue Jackets in the 1st round of the 2001 entry draft. He has appeared in 816 NHL games during his career, notching 417 goals and adding 400 assists along with 516 penalty minutes. The 34 year old left wing looks to stay a member of the team for a long time.
