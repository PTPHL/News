6-2-2020 Patrice Bergeron reaches a milestone !

A native of Canada, Patrice Bergeron was originally drafted to the NHL by the Boston Bruins in the 2nd round of the 2003 entry draft. He has appeared in 1117 NHL games during his career, notching 318 goals and adding 500 assists along with 456 penalty minutes.
