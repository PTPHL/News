7-1-2018 Tampa Bay Lightning trade
Tampa Bay Lightning traded Miles Wood and Mikhail Sergachev to Winnipeg Jets for Joshua Morrissey and Kyle Connor

A native of Canada, Joshua Morrissey was originally drafted to the NHL by the Columbus Blue Jackets in the 1st round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 127 NHL games during his career, notching 7 goals and adding 20 assists along with 56 penalty minutes. The 22 year old defenseman has played 44 games with the Winnipeg Jets this season, totalling 1+6=7 with 18 PIM.

A native of USA, Kyle Connor was originally drafted to the NHL by the Columbus Blue Jackets in the 1st round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 64 NHL games during his career, notching 13 goals and adding 25 assists along with 34 penalty minutes. The 21 year old left wing has played 44 games with the Winnipeg Jets this season, totalling 11+22=33 with 30 PIM.

A native of USA, Miles Wood was originally drafted to the NHL by the New Jersey Devils in the 4th round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 103 NHL games during his career, notching 10 goals and adding 15 assists along with 128 penalty minutes. The 22 year old left wing has played 42 games with the Tampa Bay Lightning this season, totalling 2+6=8 with 42 PIM.

A native of Russia, Mikhail Sergachev was originally drafted to the NHL by the Montreal Canadiens in the 1st round of the 2016 entry draft. He has appeared in 46 NHL games during his career, notching 4 goals and adding 16 assists along with 36 penalty minutes. The 19 year old defenseman has played 42 games with the Tampa Bay Lightning this season, totalling 4+16=20 with 36 PIM.
