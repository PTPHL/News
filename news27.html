4-2-2018 Daniel Walcott frustrated
Tampa Bay Lightning defenseman Daniel Walcott feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Daniel Walcott was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Rangers in the 5th round of the 2014 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old defenseman has not yet played for the Tampa Bay Lightning this season. He has played 55 games with the Syracuse Crunch this season, totalling 4+8=12 with 16 PIM.
3-2-2018 Player of the week: Tyler Seguin (C)
NHL has awarded Tyler Seguin (C) of Tampa Bay Lightning as Player Of The Week He registered 4 goals and 4 assists for 8 points in 4 games last week.

A native of Canada, Tyler Seguin was originally drafted to the NHL by the Boston Bruins in the 1st round of the 2010 entry draft. He has appeared in 562 NHL games during his career, notching 212 goals and adding 266 assists along with 156 penalty minutes. The 26 year old center has played 54 games with the Tampa Bay Lightning this season, totalling 23+28=51 with 16 PIM.
