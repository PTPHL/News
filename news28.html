23-9-2017 David Backes frustrated
Toronto Maple Leafs right wing David Backes feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, David Backes was originally drafted to the NHL by the St. Louis Blues in the 2nd round of the 2003 entry draft. He has appeared in 808 NHL games during his career, notching 224 goals and adding 277 assists along with 1046 penalty minutes. The 33 year old right wing has played 7 games with the Toronto Maple Leafs this season, totalling 1+2=3 with 8 PIM.
