31-8-2017 Dustin Byfuglien frustrated
Toronto Maple Leafs defenseman Dustin Byfuglien feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Dustin Byfuglien was originally drafted to the NHL by the Chicago Blackhawks in the 8th round of the 2003 entry draft. He has appeared in 758 NHL games during his career, notching 165 goals and adding 284 assists along with 913 penalty minutes. The 32 year old defenseman is waiting for a good start this season.
