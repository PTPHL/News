1-12-2017 Player of the month: David Backes (R)
NHL has awarded David Backes (R) of Toronto Maple Leafs as Player Of The Month He registered 10 goals and 6 assists for 16 points in 14 games last month. He also had 3 game winning goals.

A native of USA, David Backes was originally drafted to the NHL by the St. Louis Blues in the 2nd round of the 2003 entry draft. He has appeared in 826 NHL games during his career, notching 236 goals and adding 285 assists along with 1080 penalty minutes. The 33 year old right wing has played 25 games with the Toronto Maple Leafs this season, totalling 13+10=23 with 42 PIM.
