7-2-2020 Nick Ritchie frustrated
Anaheim Ducks right wing Nick Ritchie feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Nick Ritchie was originally drafted to the NHL by the Anaheim Ducks in the 1st round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 328 NHL games during his career, notching 51 goals and adding 64 assists along with 254 penalty minutes. The 24 year old right wing has played 54 games with the Anaheim Ducks this season, totalling 9+14=23 with 72 PIM.
