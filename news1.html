4-12-2017 Kevin Roy frustrated
Anaheim Ducks left wing Kevin Roy feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Kevin Roy was originally drafted to the NHL by the Anaheim Ducks in the 4th round of the 2012 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 24 year old left wing has not yet played for the Anaheim Ducks this season. He has played 28 games with the San Diego Gulls this season, totalling 5+2=7 with 2 PIM.
3-12-2017 Logan Shaw reaches career high !
Logan Shaw marked a new career high in points with 11 after scoring in tonights game against Los Angeles Kings on a feed by Adam Henrique. You've got to hand it to the other guys in the room though, they gave it their all tonight, he commented during a post game radio interview.

A native of Canada, Logan Shaw was originally drafted to the NHL by the Florida Panthers in the 3rd round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 135 NHL games during his career, notching 15 goals and adding 13 assists along with 25 penalty minutes. The 25 year old right wing has played 27 games with the Anaheim Ducks this season, totalling 7+4=11 with 2 PIM.
