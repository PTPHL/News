4-2-2020 Matthew Bradley frustrated
New York Islanders center Matthew Bradley feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Matthew Bradley was originally drafted to the NHL by the Montreal Canadiens in the 5th round of the 2015 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old center has not yet played for the New York Islanders this season. He has played 23 games with the Bridgeport Sound Tigers this season, totalling 2+1=3 with 10 PIM.
