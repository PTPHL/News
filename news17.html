6-2-2018 Adam Pelech reaches career high !
Adam Pelech marked a new career high in points with 11 after scoring in tonights game against Montreal Canadiens on a feed by Dougie Hamilton. Yes, it does feel great, he commented to the media.

A native of Canada, Adam Pelech was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Islanders in the 3rd round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 105 NHL games during his career, notching 4 goals and adding 19 assists along with 10 penalty minutes. The 23 year old defenseman has played 52 games with the New York Islanders this season, totalling 1+10=11 with 4 PIM.

4-2-2018 Anders Lee reaches a milestone !
Anders Lee scored the 100th goal of his career in tonight's game against the Pittsburgh Penguins on a feed by Brock Nelson. I just try and give it my best out there. This one was special though, was his only statement before going to the dressing room.

A native of USA, Anders Lee was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Islanders in the 6th round of the 2009 entry draft. He has appeared in 312 NHL games during his career, notching 100 goals and adding 89 assists along with 216 penalty minutes. The 27 year old right wing has played 51 games with the New York Islanders this season, totalling 16+28=44 with 62 PIM.
