30-1-2018 Alex Ovechkin reaches a milestone !
Alex Ovechkin notched the 500th assist of his career in tonight's game against the Florida Panthers on a goal by Evgeni Kuznetsov. For me this is dream come true, our record breaker told a Hockey Night in Canada reporter..

A native of Russia, Alex Ovechkin was originally drafted to the NHL by the Washington Capitals in the 1st round of the 2004 entry draft. He has appeared in 972 NHL games during his career, notching 573 goals and adding 500 assists along with 653 penalty minutes. The 32 year old left wing has played 51 games with the Washington Capitals this season, totalling 15+23=38 with 36 PIM.
