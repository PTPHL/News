6-1-2018 Weekly team report
Who's hot

Max Domi is looking steady with 3 points in 3 games last week. Nicklas Bäckström is looking good with 4 points in 3 games last week. Alex Ovechkin is looking good with 4 points in 3 games last week. Evgeni Kuznetsov is looking good with 4 points in 3 games last week. Tom Wilson is looking red hot with a 9 game point streak.

Who's cold

Michal Kempny has played 12 games without a goal. Matt Niskanen has played 10 games without a goal. Brett Connolly has lost his sights and is now 12 games without a goal. Chandler Stephenson is down on his game pulling a 10 game streak without registering a single point. Devante Smith-Pelly has lost his sights and is now 7 games without a goal. Michael Hutchinson hasn't got a chance to start in the last five games. James Wisniewski has played 39 games without a goal.

Trainer's room

André Burakovsky remains sidelined with abdomen injury. Simon Després remains sidelined with groin injury.


5-1-2018 Ron Wilson praises Zack Malik
Washington Capitals head coach Ron Wilson has praised young defenseman Zack Malik in his latest interviews. Altough he seems very fond of this highly skilled youngster, he didn't want to comment on his team's strategy concerning the upcoming draft.

Zack Malik has played 27 games with HC Vitkovice this season, collecting 4+7=11 with 5 PIM.

4-1-2018 Adam Carlson frustrated
Washington Capitals goaltender Adam Carlson feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Adam Carlson, was originally signed as a free agent to the NHL. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old goaltender has not yet played for the Washington Capitals this season.
4-1-2018 Washington Capitals trade rumoured
Buffalo Sabres are rumoured to be planning a trade sending center Tyler Steenbergen and right wing Victor Olofsson to Washington Capitals for left wing Max Domi and WAS 3rd round pick in 2019 draft

Rumours from various sources around the league say that the Buffalo Sabres are looking for a 1st line left wing and that Max Domi would be a good fit there.
3-1-2018 Brett Connolly injured.
Brett Connolly (WAS) suffered ankle injury during the game in an incident with Justin Faulk (CAR). He is expected to be sidelined for a couple of days. This is a rough game and injuries happen, lucky for me that this one is not serious, the injured player commented.
