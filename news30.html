Nicklas Bäckström scored the 200th goal of his career in tonight's game against the Winnipeg Jets on a feed by Alex Ovechkin. I can't quite remember the last time when I was this happy, he said to the press.

A native of Sweden, Nicklas Bäckström was originally drafted to the NHL by the Washington Capitals in the 1st round of the 2006 entry draft. He has appeared in 758 NHL games during his career, notching 200 goals and adding 550 assists along with 380 penalty minutes. The 30 year old center has played 24 games with the Washington Capitals this season, totalling 12+10=22 with 8 PIM.
