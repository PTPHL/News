8-2-2018 Jujhar Khaira frustrated
Detroit Red Wings left wing Jujhar Khaira feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Jujhar Khaira was originally drafted to the NHL by the Edmonton Oilers in the 3rd round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 81 NHL games during his career, notching 3 goals and adding 6 assists along with 33 penalty minutes. The 23 year old left wing has played 56 games with the Detroit Red Wings this season, totalling 2+4=6 with 18 PIM.
8-2-2018 Christian Dvorak reaches career high !
Christian Dvorak marked a new career high in assists with 19 assists after assisting in tonights game against Nashville Predators on a goal by Anthony Mantha. I'm just happy to contribute, he commented and was all smiles.

A native of USA, Christian Dvorak was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vegas Golden Knights in the 2nd round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 132 NHL games during his career, notching 33 goals and adding 37 assists along with 44 penalty minutes. The 22 year old left wing has played 54 games with the Detroit Red Wings this season, totalling 18+19=37 with 22 PIM.

4-2-2018 Anthony Mantha reaches career high !
Anthony Mantha marked a new career high in goals with 18 tallies after scoring in tonights game against Nashville Predators on a feed by Mike Green. I wouldn't have been able to do this without my great teammates, he commented during a post game radio interview.

A native of Canada, Anthony Mantha was originally drafted to the NHL by the Detroit Red Wings in the 1st round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 122 NHL games during his career, notching 37 goals and adding 44 assists along with 87 penalty minutes. The 23 year old right wing has played 52 games with the Detroit Red Wings this season, totalling 18+24=42 with 32 PIM.
