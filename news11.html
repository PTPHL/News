7-1-2018 Christian Dvorak reaches career high !
Christian Dvorak marked a new career high in points with 34 after scoring in tonights game against Edmonton Oilers on a feed by Anthony Mantha. The puck has been bouncing for me lately, he replied before ducking into the dressing room.

A native of USA, Christian Dvorak was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vegas Golden Knights in the 2nd round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 120 NHL games during his career, notching 32 goals and adding 35 assists along with 40 penalty minutes. The 21 year old left wing has played 42 games with the Detroit Red Wings this season, totalling 17+17=34 with 18 PIM.
