12-9-2017 Tuukka Rask frustrated
Detroit Red Wings goaltender Tuukka Rask feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Finland, Tuukka Rask was originally drafted to the NHL by the Toronto Maple Leafs in the 1st round of the 2005 entry draft. He has appeared in 395 NHL games during his career, booking 38 career shutouts and allowing 844 goals. The 30 year old goaltender has not yet played for the Detroit Red Wings this season.
