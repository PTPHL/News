9-12-2017 Dominic Toninato frustrated
Colorado Avalanche left wing Dominic Toninato feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Dominic Toninato was originally drafted to the NHL by the Toronto Maple Leafs in the 5th round of the 2012 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level.
