7-1-2018 Lawson Crouse reaches career high !
Lawson Crouse marked a new career high in points with 13 after assisting in tonights game against Winnipeg Jets on a goal by Mikko Rantanen. This one is for the fans who are paying my salary, he said to the press..

A native of Canada, Lawson Crouse was originally drafted to the NHL by the Florida Panthers in the 1st round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 83 NHL games during his career, notching 6 goals and adding 19 assists along with 52 penalty minutes. The 20 year old left wing has played 11 games with the Colorado Avalanche this season, totalling 1+12=13 with 4 PIM. He has also played 30 games with the Colorado Eagles this season, totalling 8+6=14 with 16 PIM.
