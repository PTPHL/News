7-11-2017 Alexander Kerfoot frustrated
Colorado Avalanche center Alexander Kerfoot feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Alexander Kerfoot was originally drafted to the NHL by the New Jersey Devils in the 5th round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 15 NHL games during his career, notching 2 goals and adding 5 assists along with 8 penalty minutes. The 23 year old center has played 15 games with the Colorado Avalanche this season, totalling 2+5=7 with 8 PIM.
