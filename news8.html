7-2-2018 Matt Nieto frustrated
Colorado Avalanche right wing Matt Nieto feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of USA, Matt Nieto was originally drafted to the NHL by the San Jose Sharks in the 2nd round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 306 NHL games during his career, notching 41 goals and adding 49 assists along with 80 penalty minutes. The 25 year old right wing has played 42 games with the Colorado Avalanche this season, totalling 6+3=9 with 26 PIM.

1-2-2018 Sven Andrighetto reaches career high !
Sven Andrighetto marked a new career high in goals with 8 tallies after scoring in tonights game against Vancouver Canucks on a feed by Tyson Jost. It's always nice score but this was one special goal for me, the happy player commented.

A native of Switzerland, Sven Andrighetto was originally drafted to the NHL by the Montreal Canadiens in the 3rd round of the 2013 entry draft. The 24 year old right wing has played 41 games with the Colorado Avalanche this season, totalling 8+11=19 with 44 PIM.
