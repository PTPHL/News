28-9-2017 Markus Granlund frustrated
Vancouver Canucks center Markus Granlund feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Finland, Markus Granlund was originally drafted to the NHL by the Calgary Flames in the 2nd round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 179 NHL games during his career, notching 35 goals and adding 28 assists along with 48 penalty minutes. The 24 year old center has played 8 games with the Vancouver Canucks this season, totalling 0+0=0 with 4 PIM.
