1-11-2017 Daniel Sedin reaches a milestone !
Daniel Sedin scored the 1000th point of his career in tonight's game against the Colorado Avalanche on a goal by Brock Boeser. Yes, it's nice to break records but that's not the idea of this game, he commented to the media outside his stall..

A native of Sweden, Daniel Sedin was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vancouver Canucks in the 1st round of the 1999 entry draft. He has appeared in 1236 NHL games during his career, notching 377 goals and adding 623 assists along with 514 penalty minutes. The 37 year old left wing has played 11 games with the Vancouver Canucks this season, totalling 7+7=14 with 8 PIM.
