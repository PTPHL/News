14-1-2018 Jake Virtanen reaches career high !
Jake Virtanen marked a new career high in points with 14 after assisting in tonights game against Minnesota Wild on a goal by Henrik Sedin. I've seen my idols break records and now I am doing the same, he replied before ducking into the dressing room..

A native of Canada, Jake Virtanen was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vancouver Canucks in the 1st round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 84 NHL games during his career, notching 12 goals and adding 16 assists along with 63 penalty minutes. The 21 year old right wing has played 19 games with the Vancouver Canucks this season, totalling 5+9=14 with 16 PIM. He has also played 25 games with the Utica Comets this season, totalling 10+13=23 with 6 PIM.
