10-2-2020 Brendan Gaunce reaches career high !
Brendan Gaunce marked a new career high in assists with 12 assists after assisting in tonights game against Calgary Flames on a goal by Michael McCarron. It's always nice to give a good pass..., he commented and was all smiles.

A native of Canada, Brendan Gaunce was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vancouver Canucks in the 1st round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 297 NHL games during his career, notching 30 goals and adding 35 assists along with 123 penalty minutes. The 25 year old left wing has played 56 games with the Vancouver Canucks this season, totalling 12+12=24 with 34 PIM.
9-2-2020 Jalen Chatfield frustrated
Vancouver Canucks defenseman Jalen Chatfield feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.
