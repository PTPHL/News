8-2-2018 Brendan Leipsic frustrated
Vancouver Canucks left wing Brendan Leipsic feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Brendan Leipsic was originally drafted to the NHL by the Nashville Predators in the 3rd round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 62 NHL games during his career, notching 5 goals and adding 11 assists along with 32 penalty minutes. The 23 year old left wing has played 56 games with the Vancouver Canucks this season, totalling 4+9=13 with 30 PIM.

4-2-2018 Jake Virtanen reaches career high !
Jake Virtanen marked a new career high in goals with 8 tallies after scoring in tonights game against Minnesota Wild on a feed by Henrik Sedin. Hopefully one day this puck will end up in the Hall of Fame, he commented and was all smiles.

A native of Canada, Jake Virtanen was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vancouver Canucks in the 1st round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 94 NHL games during his career, notching 15 goals and adding 19 assists along with 75 penalty minutes. The 21 year old right wing has played 29 games with the Vancouver Canucks this season, totalling 8+12=20 with 28 PIM. He has also played 25 games with the Utica Comets this season, totalling 10+13=23 with 6 PIM.
