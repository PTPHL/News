6-2-2018 Matthew Tkachuk reaches career high !
Matthew Tkachuk marked a new career high in goals with 14 tallies after scoring in tonights game against Washington Capitals on a feed by Joshua Ho-Sang. It's always nice score but this was one special goal for me, the happy player commented.

A native of USA, Matthew Tkachuk was originally drafted to the NHL by the Calgary Flames in the 1st round of the 2016 entry draft. He has appeared in 128 NHL games during his career, notching 27 goals and adding 60 assists along with 121 penalty minutes. The 20 year old left wing has played 52 games with the Calgary Flames this season, totalling 14+25=39 with 16 PIM.
