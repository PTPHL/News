10-2-2020 Oliver Kylington reaches career high !
Oliver Kylington marked a new career high in assists with 25 assists after assisting in tonights game against Vancouver Canucks on a goal by Johnny Gaudreau. The pass just found the right guy, what more can I say, he said as he packed up his gear.

A native of Sweden, Oliver Kylington was originally drafted to the NHL by the Calgary Flames in the 2nd round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 138 NHL games during his career, notching 8 goals and adding 49 assists along with 43 penalty minutes. The 22 year old defenseman has played 55 games with the Calgary Flames this season, totalling 2+25=27 with 28 PIM.
