31-1-2018 Marian Gaborik reaches a milestone !
Marian Gaborik scored the 400th goal of his career in tonight's game against the Florida Panthers on a feed by Ryan Dzingel. It's not so big but I'm still very happy about it, he said before boarding the teams chartered jet.

A native of Slovakia, Marian Gaborik was originally drafted to the NHL by the Minnesota Wild in the 1st round of the 2000 entry draft. He has appeared in 1038 NHL games during his career, notching 400 goals and adding 402 assists along with 492 penalty minutes. The 35 year old right wing has played 49 games with the Ottawa Senators this season, totalling 4+4=8 with 24 PIM.
