12-1-2018 Fredrik Claesson frustrated
Ottawa Senators defenseman Fredrik Claesson feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Sweden, Fredrik Claesson was originally drafted to the NHL by the Ottawa Senators in the 5th round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 85 NHL games during his career, notching 5 goals and adding 20 assists along with 16 penalty minutes. The 25 year old defenseman has played 36 games with the Ottawa Senators this season, totalling 2+10=12 with 10 PIM. He has also played 7 games with the Bellville Senators this season, totalling 1+0=1 with 2 PIM.

1-1-2018 Marian Gaborik reaches a milestone !
Marian Gaborik scored the 800th point of his career in tonight's game against the Boston Bruins on a goal by Ryan Dzingel. It is just part of my job, he commented during a post game radio interview..

A native of Slovakia, Marian Gaborik was originally drafted to the NHL by the Minnesota Wild in the 1st round of the 2000 entry draft. He has appeared in 1025 NHL games during his career, notching 399 goals and adding 401 assists along with 486 penalty minutes. The 35 year old right wing has played 36 games with the Ottawa Senators this season, totalling 3+3=6 with 18 PIM.
