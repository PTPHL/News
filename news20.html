I wish I could contribute, but I'll be out of action for awhile. Hopefully some of the others can step up their play a bit in the meantime, were his words after the game.
6-11-2017 Emil Pettersson frustrated
New Jersey Devils center Emil Pettersson feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Sweden, Emil Pettersson was originally drafted to the NHL by the Nashville Predators in the 6th round of the 2013 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 23 year old center has not yet played for the New Jersey Devils this season. He has played 14 games with the Binghamton Devils this season, totalling 1+2=3 with 2 PIM.

1-11-2017 Rookie of the month: Nico Hischier (L)
NHL has awarded Nico Hischier (L) of New Jersey Devils as Rookie Of The Month He registered 6 goals and 6 assists for 12 points in 9 games last month. He also had 1 game winning goal.

A native of Switzerland, Nico Hischier was originally drafted to the NHL by the New Jersey Devils in the 1st round of the 2017 entry draft. He has appeared in 9 NHL games during his career, notching 6 goals and adding 6 assists along with 2 penalty minutes. The 18 year old left wing has played 9 games with the New Jersey Devils this season, totalling 6+6=12 with 2 PIM.
