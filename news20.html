14-9-2017 Mathieu Perreault frustrated
New Jersey Devils left wing Mathieu Perreault feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Mathieu Perreault was originally drafted to the NHL by the Washington Capitals in the 6th round of the 2006 entry draft. He has appeared in 426 NHL games during his career, notching 91 goals and adding 149 assists along with 210 penalty minutes. The 29 year old left wing has not yet played for the New Jersey Devils this season.
11-9-2017 Pierre-Olivier Joseph injured.
Pierre-Olivier Joseph (NJD) suffered head injury during the game in an incident with Chris Kreider (NYR). He is expected to be sidelined for a couple of days. If it was up to me I would have finished the game, but they want to be careful about this kind of injury, he quoted before being taken to the hospital for tests.
