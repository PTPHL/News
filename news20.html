1-12-2017 Rookie of the month: Nico Hischier (L)
NHL has awarded Nico Hischier (L) of New Jersey Devils as Rookie Of The Month He registered 9 goals and 2 assists for 11 points in 14 games last month. He also had 1 game winning goal.

A native of Switzerland, Nico Hischier was originally drafted to the NHL by the New Jersey Devils in the 1st round of the 2017 entry draft. He has appeared in 23 NHL games during his career, notching 15 goals and adding 8 assists along with 8 penalty minutes. The 18 year old left wing has played 23 games with the New Jersey Devils this season, totalling 15+8=23 with 8 PIM.
