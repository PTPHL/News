14-10-2017 Kyle Palmieri reaches a milestone !
Kyle Palmieri scored the 100th goal of his career in tonight's game against the Minnesota Wild on a feed by Tyler Johnson. It is just an amazing thing for me, he said outside the teams dressing room.

A native of USA, Kyle Palmieri was originally drafted to the NHL by the Anaheim Ducks in the 1st round of the 2009 entry draft. He has appeared in 362 NHL games during his career, notching 100 goals and adding 101 assists along with 177 penalty minutes. The 26 year old right wing has played 2 games with the New Jersey Devils this season, totalling 1+1=2 with 2 PIM.
