21-2-2018 Ryan Hartman reaches career high !
Ryan Hartman marked a new career high in assists with 13 assists after assisting in tonights game against Tampa Bay Lightning on a goal by P.K. Subban. You gotta' hand it to their team today, they came to play and made us work for everything we got, he read from a prepared speech.

A native of USA, Ryan Hartman was originally drafted to the NHL by the Chicago Blackhawks in the 1st round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 146 NHL games during his career, notching 25 goals and adding 26 assists along with 114 penalty minutes. The 23 year old left wing has played 62 games with the Nashville Predators this season, totalling 6+13=19 with 42 PIM.
