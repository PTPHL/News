13-1-2018 Player of the week: Kyle Turris (C)
NHL has awarded Kyle Turris (C) of Nashville Predators as Player Of The Week He registered 3 goals and 2 assists for 5 points in 4 games last week. He also had 2 game winning goals.

A native of Canada, Kyle Turris was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vegas Golden Knights in the 1st round of the 2007 entry draft. He has appeared in 579 NHL games during his career, notching 151 goals and adding 199 assists along with 282 penalty minutes. The 28 year old center has played 46 games with the Nashville Predators this season, totalling 18+21=39 with 34 PIM.
