8-2-2018 Viktor Arvidsson reaches career high !
Viktor Arvidsson marked a new career high in assists with 31 assists after assisting in tonights game against Detroit Red Wings on a goal by Christian Fischer. It's a joy to be playing with these guys, they are the best teammates anyone could have, he commented to the media.

A native of Sweden, Viktor Arvidsson was originally drafted to the NHL by the Nashville Predators in the 4th round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 199 NHL games during his career, notching 59 goals and adding 69 assists along with 113 penalty minutes. The 24 year old left wing has played 57 games with the Nashville Predators this season, totalling 20+31=51 with 50 PIM.
