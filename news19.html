15-9-2017 Filip Forsberg injured.
Filip Forsberg (NAS) suffered chest injury during the game in an incident with Nikita Zaitsev (WPG). He is expected to be sidelined for at least four months. It's a bad injury for the team, but the guys just can't go out there and give up. Hopefully they will take this opportunity to pick up their play a bit in my absence, he quoted before being taken to the hospital for tests.
9-9-2017 Player of the week: Viktor Arvidsson (R)
NHL has awarded Viktor Arvidsson (R) of Nashville Predators as Player Of The Week He registered 2 goals and 2 assists for 4 points in 2 games last week. He also had 1 game winning goal.

A native of Sweden, Viktor Arvidsson was originally drafted to the NHL by the Nashville Predators in the 4th round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 144 NHL games during his career, notching 41 goals and adding 40 assists along with 65 penalty minutes. The 24 year old right wing has played 2 games with the Nashville Predators this season, totalling 2+2=4 with 2 PIM.
