3-11-2017 Filip Forsberg frustrated
Nashville Predators left wing Filip Forsberg feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Sweden, Filip Forsberg was originally drafted to the NHL by the Washington Capitals in the 1st round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 264 NHL games during his career, notching 91 goals and adding 100 assists along with 107 penalty minutes. The 23 year old left wing has not yet played for the Nashville Predators this season.
