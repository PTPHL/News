10-2-2020 Dante Fabbro reaches career high !
Dante Fabbro marked a new career high in assists with 14 assists after assisting in tonights game against Winnipeg Jets on a goal by Viktor Arvidsson. The pass just found the right guy, what more can I say, were his words after the game.

A native of Canada, Dante Fabbro was originally drafted to the NHL by the Nashville Predators in the 1st round of the 2016 entry draft. He has appeared in 145 NHL games during his career, notching 8 goals and adding 28 assists along with 81 penalty minutes. The 21 year old defenseman has played 58 games with the Nashville Predators this season, totalling 5+14=19 with 59 PIM.
