13-2-2020 Jared McCann frustrated
Boston Bruins center Jared McCann feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Jared McCann was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vancouver Canucks in the 1st round of the 2014 entry draft. He has appeared in 230 NHL games during his career, notching 25 goals and adding 39 assists along with 90 penalty minutes. The 23 year old center has not yet played for the Boston Bruins this season.
