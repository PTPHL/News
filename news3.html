12-1-2018 Riley Nash frustrated
Boston Bruins center Riley Nash feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Riley Nash was originally drafted to the NHL by the Edmonton Oilers in the 1st round of the 2007 entry draft. He has appeared in 366 NHL games during his career, notching 41 goals and adding 62 assists along with 91 penalty minutes. The 28 year old center has played 43 games with the Boston Bruins this season, totalling 3+2=5 with 8 PIM.
