5-2-2018 David Krejci frustrated
Boston Bruins center David Krejci feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Czech, David Krejci was originally drafted to the NHL by the Boston Bruins in the 2nd round of the 2004 entry draft. He has appeared in 760 NHL games during his career, notching 169 goals and adding 392 assists along with 288 penalty minutes. The 31 year old center has played 55 games with the Boston Bruins this season, totalling 12+23=35 with 22 PIM.
