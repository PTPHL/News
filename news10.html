12-1-2018 Nikita Filatov frustrated
Dallas Stars left wing Nikita Filatov feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Russia, Nikita Filatov was originally drafted to the NHL by the Winnipeg Jets in the 1st round of the 2008 entry draft. He has appeared in 90 NHL games during his career, notching 12 goals and adding 18 assists along with 48 penalty minutes. The 27 year old left wing has played 37 games with the Dallas Stars this season, totalling 6+10=16 with 28 PIM.
10-1-2018 Team captain meets with Esa Lindell
Team captain Connor McDavid called a short meeting with Esa Lindell after practice today to discuss some issues that have affected his play. I have to take the brunt of this, said Esa Lindell as he left the ice. My play has not been up to snuff and if I'm not contributing more, I have to work harder.
