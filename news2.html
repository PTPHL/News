They're being cautious about my condition, but I feel pretty good right now and want to get back on the ice as soon as possible, was his answer to a reporter question after the game.
1-11-2017 Nick Foligno frustrated
Columbus Blue Jackets left wing Nick Foligno feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of USA, Nick Foligno was originally drafted to the NHL by the Ottawa Senators in the 1st round of the 2006 entry draft. He has appeared in 705 NHL games during his career, notching 155 goals and adding 217 assists along with 585 penalty minutes. The 30 year old left wing has played 9 games with the Columbus Blue Jackets this season, totalling 1+4=5 with 4 PIM.
