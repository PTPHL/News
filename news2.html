22-6-2017 Zac Dalpe frustrated
Columbus Blue Jackets right wing Zac Dalpe feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Zac Dalpe was originally drafted to the NHL by the Carolina Hurricanes in the 2nd round of the 2008 entry draft. He has appeared in 128 NHL games during his career, notching 12 goals and adding 12 assists along with 23 penalty minutes. The 27 year old right wing is eagerly waiting to hit the ice once the season starts.
