6-1-2018 Erik Haula reaches career high !
Erik Haula marked a new career high in goals with 16 tallies after scoring in tonights game against Buffalo Sabres on a feed by Alex Galchenyuk. The coach gave me a chance to play, and I'm just trying to show him he made the right decision, he said outside the teams dressing room.

A native of Finland, Erik Haula was originally drafted to the NHL by the Minnesota Wild in the 7th round of the 2009 entry draft. He has appeared in 305 NHL games during his career, notching 58 goals and adding 63 assists along with 131 penalty minutes. The 26 year old center has played 39 games with the Montreal Canadiens this season, totalling 16+16=32 with 18 PIM.
5-1-2018 Colin Miller frustrated
Montreal Canadiens defenseman Colin Miller feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Colin Miller was originally drafted to the NHL by the Los Angeles Kings in the 5th round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 142 NHL games during his career, notching 15 goals and adding 36 assists along with 116 penalty minutes. The 25 year old defenseman has played 39 games with the Montreal Canadiens this season, totalling 6+16=22 with 22 PIM.
