1-9-2017 Jason Demers frustrated
Montreal Canadiens defenseman Jason Demers feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Jason Demers was originally drafted to the NHL by the San Jose Sharks in the 7th round of the 2008 entry draft. He has appeared in 504 NHL games during his career, notching 37 goals and adding 134 assists along with 307 penalty minutes. The 29 year old defenseman is eagerly waiting to hit the ice once the season starts.
