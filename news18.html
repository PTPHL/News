4-11-2017 Player of the week: Kevin Hayes (C)
NHL has awarded Kevin Hayes (C) of New York Rangers as Player Of The Week He registered 4 goals and 1 assists for 5 points in 4 games last week. He also had 2 game winning goals.

A native of USA, Kevin Hayes was originally drafted to the NHL by the Chicago Blackhawks in the 1st round of the 2010 entry draft. He has appeared in 247 NHL games during his career, notching 53 goals and adding 85 assists along with 76 penalty minutes. The 25 year old center has played 13 games with the New York Rangers this season, totalling 5+3=8 with 6 PIM.
