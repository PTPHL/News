6-1-2018 Pavel Buchnevich reaches career high !
Pavel Buchnevich marked a new career high in goals with 9 tallies after scoring in tonights game against New York Islanders on a feed by Vladislav Namestnikov. I just try and give it my best out there. This one was special though, he commented during a post game radio interview.

A native of Russia, Pavel Buchnevich was originally drafted to the NHL by the New York Rangers in the 3rd round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 81 NHL games during his career, notching 17 goals and adding 21 assists along with 33 penalty minutes. The 22 year old right wing has played 40 games with the New York Rangers this season, totalling 9+9=18 with 20 PIM.
