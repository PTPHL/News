5-9-2017 Ryan Spooner frustrated
New York Rangers left wing Ryan Spooner feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Ryan Spooner was originally drafted to the NHL by the Boston Bruins in the 2nd round of the 2010 entry draft. He has appeared in 214 NHL games during his career, notching 32 goals and adding 85 assists along with 57 penalty minutes. The 25 year old left wing has not yet played for the New York Rangers this season.
