1-1-2018 Mikkel Boedker reaches a milestone !
Mikkel Boedker scored the 100th goal of his career in tonight's game against the Dallas Stars on a feed by Logan Couture. We have a good team this year, I'm just happy I can help out, he commented after giving the puck to a fan.

A native of La-la-land, Mikkel Boedker was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vegas Golden Knights in the 1st round of the 2008 entry draft. He has appeared in 580 NHL games during his career, notching 100 goals and adding 168 assists along with 116 penalty minutes. The 28 year old left wing has played 36 games with the San Jose Sharks this season, totalling 6+11=17 with 18 PIM.
