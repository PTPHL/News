30-11-2017 Joe Pavelski frustrated
San Jose Sharks center Joe Pavelski feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a franchise player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a core player.

A native of USA, Joe Pavelski was originally drafted to the NHL by the San Jose Sharks in the 7th round of the 2003 entry draft. He has appeared in 828 NHL games during his career, notching 301 goals and adding 345 assists along with 314 penalty minutes. The 33 year old center has played 22 games with the San Jose Sharks this season, totalling 6+9=15 with 6 PIM.
