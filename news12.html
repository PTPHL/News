3-2-2020 Bo Horvat frustrated
Edmonton Oilers center Bo Horvat feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a franchise player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Bo Horvat was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vancouver Canucks in the 1st round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 418 NHL games during his career, notching 88 goals and adding 127 assists along with 121 penalty minutes. The 24 year old center has played 51 games with the Edmonton Oilers this season, totalling 10+14=24 with 8 PIM.
