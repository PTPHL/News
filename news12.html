28-9-2017 Jujhar Khaira frustrated
Edmonton Oilers left wing Jujhar Khaira feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a regular player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Canada, Jujhar Khaira was originally drafted to the NHL by the Edmonton Oilers in the 3rd round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 25 NHL games during his career, notching 1 goals and adding 2 assists along with 15 penalty minutes. The 23 year old left wing has not yet played for the Edmonton Oilers this season.
