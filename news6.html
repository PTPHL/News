5-1-2018 Brock Mcginn frustrated
Carolina Hurricanes right wing Brock Mcginn feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Brock Mcginn was originally drafted to the NHL by the Carolina Hurricanes in the 2nd round of the 2012 entry draft. He has appeared in 115 NHL games during his career, notching 14 goals and adding 15 assists along with 44 penalty minutes. The 23 year old right wing has played 37 games with the Carolina Hurricanes this season, totalling 4+5=9 with 28 PIM.
