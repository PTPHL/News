7-2-2020 Jordan Staal reaches a milestone !
Jordan Staal notched the 300th assist of his career in tonight's game against the Colorado Avalanche on a goal by Andrew Ladd. For me this is dream come true, he said after the game..

A native of Canada, Jordan Staal was originally drafted to the NHL by the Pittsburgh Penguins in the 1st round of the 2006 entry draft. He has appeared in 982 NHL games during his career, notching 218 goals and adding 300 assists along with 598 penalty minutes. The 31 year old center has played 55 games with the Carolina Hurricanes this season, totalling 10+16=26 with 26 PIM.
