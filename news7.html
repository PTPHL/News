1-12-2017 Tomas Jurco frustrated
Chicago Blackhawks left wing Tomas Jurco feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Slovakia, Tomas Jurco was originally drafted to the NHL by the Detroit Red Wings in the 2nd round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 187 NHL games during his career, notching 16 goals and adding 24 assists along with 50 penalty minutes. The 24 year old left wing has played 15 games with the Chicago Blackhawks this season, totalling 0+0=0 with 2 PIM. He has also played 11 games with the Rockford IceHogs this season, totalling 7+0=7 with 10 PIM.
