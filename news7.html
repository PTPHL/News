3-1-2018 Patrick Kane reaches a milestone !
Patrick Kane scored the 300th goal of his career in tonight's game against the Vancouver Canucks on a feed by Duncan Keith. As long as the team wins I'm happy. The personal records don't mean anything to me if they aren't a part of winning the Cup, he said outside the teams dressing room.

A native of USA, Patrick Kane was originally drafted to the NHL by the Chicago Blackhawks in the 1st round of the 2007 entry draft. He has appeared in 778 NHL games during his career, notching 300 goals and adding 496 assists along with 307 penalty minutes. The 29 year old right wing has played 38 games with the Chicago Blackhawks this season, totalling 15+29=44 with 23 PIM.
