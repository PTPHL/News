31-8-2017 Kyle Capobianco agrees on a contract.
Kyle Capobianco has signed a $750,000 per year, 3 year deal with the Chicago Blackhawks. Detailed terms of the deal were not announced.

A native of Canada, Kyle Capobianco was originally drafted to the NHL by the Vegas Golden Knights in the 3rd round of the 2015 entry draft. He has not yet played at NHL level. The 20 year old defenseman is getting ready for the upcoming season.
