Patrick Sharp scored the 600th point of his career in tonight's game against the Ottawa Senators on a goal by Patrick Kane. When you come to play, good things happen, he said after the game..

A native of Canada, Patrick Sharp was originally drafted to the NHL by the Philadelphia Flyers in the 3rd round of the 2001 entry draft. He has appeared in 869 NHL games during his career, notching 277 goals and adding 323 assists along with 522 penalty minutes. The 35 year old left wing looks to stay a member of the team for a long time.
