6-1-2018 Player of the week: Trevor Lewis (R)
NHL has awarded Trevor Lewis (R) of Los Angeles Kings as Player Of The Week He registered 5 goals and 2 assists for 7 points in 4 games last week. He also had 1 game winning goal.

A native of USA, Trevor Lewis was originally drafted to the NHL by the Los Angeles Kings in the 1st round of the 2006 entry draft. He has appeared in 548 NHL games during his career, notching 58 goals and adding 80 assists along with 157 penalty minutes. The 30 year old right wing has played 42 games with the Los Angeles Kings this season, totalling 11+14=25 with 36 PIM.
