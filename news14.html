5-12-2017 Tobias Rieder frustrated
Los Angeles Kings left wing Tobias Rieder feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Germany, Tobias Rieder was originally drafted to the NHL by the Edmonton Oilers in the 4th round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 261 NHL games during his career, notching 53 goals and adding 62 assists along with 40 penalty minutes. The 24 year old left wing has played 27 games with the Los Angeles Kings this season, totalling 10+13=23 with 10 PIM.
