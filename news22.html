2-1-2018 Travis Konecny reaches career high !
Travis Konecny marked a new career high in goals with 12 tallies after scoring in tonights game against New York Islanders on a feed by Ivan Provorov. I just feel so happy now, this comes at a good time for me, he said after the game.

A native of Canada, Travis Konecny was originally drafted to the NHL by the Philadelphia Flyers in the 1st round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 108 NHL games during his career, notching 23 goals and adding 38 assists along with 109 penalty minutes. The 20 year old right wing has played 38 games with the Philadelphia Flyers this season, totalling 12+21=33 with 60 PIM.
31-12-2017 Brandon Manning frustrated
Philadelphia Flyers defenseman Brandon Manning feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Brandon Manning, was originally signed as a free agent to the NHL. He has appeared in 180 NHL games during his career, notching 5 goals and adding 25 assists along with 196 penalty minutes. The 27 year old defenseman has played 38 games with the Philadelphia Flyers this season, totalling 1+5=6 with 40 PIM.
