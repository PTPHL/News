2-11-2017 Nick Cousins frustrated
Minnesota Wild left wing Nick Cousins feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a regular player.

A native of Canada, Nick Cousins was originally drafted to the NHL by the Philadelphia Flyers in the 3rd round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 120 NHL games during his career, notching 12 goals and adding 16 assists along with 59 penalty minutes. The 24 year old left wing has played 13 games with the Minnesota Wild this season, totalling 0+1=1 with 22 PIM.
