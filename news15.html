5-1-2018 Marko Dano frustrated
Minnesota Wild right wing Marko Dano feels frustrated about his position in the organization. He feels he's good enough to be a core player for the team and wonders why the organization still considers him a minor league player.

A native of Slovakia, Marko Dano was originally drafted to the NHL by the Winnipeg Jets in the 1st round of the 2013 entry draft. He has appeared in 118 NHL games during his career, notching 17 goals and adding 28 assists along with 36 penalty minutes. The 23 year old right wing has played 11 games with the Minnesota Wild this season, totalling 0+3=3 with 2 PIM. He has also played 26 games with the Iowa Wild this season, totalling 2+2=4 with 2 PIM.
