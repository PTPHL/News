1-1-2018 Rookie of the month: Kyle Connor (L)
NHL has awarded Kyle Connor (L) of Winnipeg Jets as Rookie Of The Month He registered 5 goals and 10 assists for 15 points in 15 games last month. He also had 1 game winning goal.

A native of USA, Kyle Connor was originally drafted to the NHL by the Columbus Blue Jackets in the 1st round of the 2015 entry draft. He has appeared in 61 NHL games during his career, notching 13 goals and adding 22 assists along with 32 penalty minutes. The 21 year old left wing has played 41 games with the Winnipeg Jets this season, totalling 11+19=30 with 28 PIM.
