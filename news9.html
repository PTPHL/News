6-2-2020 Jonas Brodin reaches career high !
Jonas Brodin marked a new career high in points with 30 after assisting in tonights game against Chicago Blackhawks on a goal by Brady Tkachuk. That's one for the books, he commented during a post game radio interview..

A native of Sweden, Jonas Brodin was originally drafted to the NHL by the Minnesota Wild in the 1st round of the 2011 entry draft. He has appeared in 543 NHL games during his career, notching 34 goals and adding 128 assists along with 175 penalty minutes. The 26 year old defenseman has played 53 games with the Winnipeg Jets this season, totalling 7+23=30 with 15 PIM.
